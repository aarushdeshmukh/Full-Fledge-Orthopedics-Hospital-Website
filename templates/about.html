Our Story

Dedicated to Orthopedic Excellence

Sonavane Hospital was established with one mission — to provide the highest quality orthopedic care to the people of Akola and surrounding regions. Over 15 years we have grown into a trusted centre of excellence for bone and joint treatment.

Our team of highly qualified orthopedic surgeons, combined with state-of-the-art infrastructure and a compassionate approach, ensures every patient receives the best possible treatment and care.

From complex joint replacements to emergency fracture care, Sonavane Hospital is equipped to handle all orthopedic needs under one roof.
